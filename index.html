About Ouroboros

Ouroboros is a community-driven organization that is established to provide a one stop solution for all kind of traders under one platform in the DeFi world.
We aim to empower our users with a comprehensive solution by providing them a platform that has benefits of a centralized exchange and beyond.

Multi Chain Dex

Connecting all isolated blockchains and establish a cross-chain asset exchange network, providing all necessary underlying support for the Defi ecosystem.

Never Ending Liquidity

Ouroboros obtains its liquidity from a variety of exchanges and has the capacity to distribute a single trade transaction across multiple DEXs in order to obtain the most competitive prices.

Community Driven

Governed by its community, via forum discussions and, when pertinent, voting on proposals by the OURO Holders.

ECOSYSTEM

Ouroboros, an innovative multi chain non-custodial decentralized exchange, elevates decentralized trading by providing users with a direct trading option through Ouroboros 's wallet app.

Wallet

Multi Chain All-in-one solution for all of your trading requirements.

Exchange

Trade your favorite crypto assets across all chains.

Aggregator

Instantly analyzes thousands of quotes and fees across multiple DEXes to provide users with the best rates.

Perpetuals Futures

Long or short, high leverage traders can trade with up to 100x leverage.

Vault

Multichain Yield Optimizer that allows its users to earn compound interest on their crypto holdings.

Lending Protocol

Lend and borrow, with pools of assets and floating interest rates algorithmically determined by the supply and demand for the asset.

DeFi &NFT Launchpad

Get early access and take part in DeFi &NFT projects across all chains.

Forums (DAO Governance)
